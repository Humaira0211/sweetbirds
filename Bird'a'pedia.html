The Robin

* Robins consume worms.

* They are often alone & are extremely territoial.

* We can enjoy their tweet all year round.

The Goldfinch

* The Goldfinch is a colourful little bird, with a bright red face.

* They are sociable birds.

* Goldfinches often migrate as far as Spain during the winter.

The Bluetit

* Blue tits eat caterpillars, nuts & seeds.

* Blue tits are found in trees.

The Sparrow

* Sparrows are very sociable birds.

* Sparrows like to live near humans.
