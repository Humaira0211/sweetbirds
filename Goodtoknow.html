Brown or white? A chicken with red earlobes will produce brown eggs, and a chicken with white earlobes will produce white eggs

Beats per minute A bird’s heart beats 400 times per minute while resting and up to 1000 beats per minute while flying.

How high? Penguins can jump as high as 6 feet in the air.

Flying asleep An albatross can sleep while it flies. It apparently dozes while cruising at 25 mph.

The most talkative bird in the world is… …the African Gray Parrot. One parrot could say over 800 words. Most species of parrots can learn only 50.

Puffin power Puffins can fly underwater.

Quack quack! A duck’s quack doesn’t echo anywhere, and no one knows why.
